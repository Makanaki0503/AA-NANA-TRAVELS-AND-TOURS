About Us

Learn about our journey, mission, and the team behind AA Nana Travels and Tours

Our Story

Founded in 2013, AA Nana Travels and Tours Ltd has grown from a small travel consultancy to one of Abuja's most trusted travel agencies. Our journey began with a simple mission: to make travel accessible, seamless, and memorable for everyone.

With over a decade of experience in the travel industry, we have built strong relationships with airlines, hotels, and tour operators worldwide. This enables us to offer our clients the best deals, exclusive packages, and personalized travel solutions.

We are a registered and licensed travel agency, committed to upholding the highest standards of professionalism and integrity. Our team of experienced travel consultants is dedicated to ensuring every journey we plan is safe, comfortable, and unforgettable.

Meet Our CEO

Nana Umar Yau brings over 15 years of experience in the travel and tourism industry. His vision for AA Nana Travels and Tours is rooted in a deep understanding of travelers' needs and a commitment to exceptional service.

"Travel is not just about reaching a destination; it's about the journey, the experiences, and the memories created along the way. At AA Nana Travels, we're committed to making every journey special."

Find answers to common questions about our services

For Umrah, you'll need:

  • Valid international passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Recent passport-sized photographs (white background)
  • Meningitis vaccination certificate
  • Completed visa application form
  • Proof of accommodation in Saudi Arabia
  • Return flight tickets

Note: Requirements may vary based on your nationality. Our team will guide you through the specific requirements for your case.

We recommend booking your Umrah package:

  • Peak seasons (Ramadan): 3-4 months in advance
  • Regular seasons: 1-2 months in advance
  • Last minute: 2-3 weeks (subject to availability)

Early booking ensures better hotel choices and flight options.

Our visa assistance process:

  1. Submit your documents (passport, photos, forms)
  2. We review and prepare your application
  3. Submit to the appropriate embassy/consulate
  4. Track your application status
  5. Collect your passport with visa

Processing times vary by destination (typically 5-15 working days).

Cancellation policy:

  • 60+ days before travel: Full refund minus administrative fee
  • 30-59 days before travel: 50% refund
  • 15-29 days before travel: 25% refund
  • Less than 15 days: No refund

We recommend travel insurance to cover unforeseen circumstances.
