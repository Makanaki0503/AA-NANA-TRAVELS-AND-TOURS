Your Responsibility: You are solely responsible for ensuring that you have:

  • A valid passport (minimum 6 months validity beyond your return date)

We provide visa assistance but do not guarantee visa approval. Visa decisions are made solely by embassies/consulates, and fees are non-refundable if a visa is denied.
